The date you start (or agree to start) bargaining. Also called the Notification Time.
The last date that employees will receive the Notice of Employee Representational Rights*
The earliest date employees can begin to vote. This must be at least:
  • 21 clear days* after the NERR date
  • 7 clear days* after the start of the access period
The start of the ‘access period’, which is the deadline to tell employees:
  • About the vote
  • How to access the agreement
The last day of voting. The vote can take place in a single day or over several weeks. An agreement is ‘made’ when a majority of employees vote to approve it.
The deadline for you to lodge your application for approval with the Commission.
